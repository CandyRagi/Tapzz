About the App

TapTheHuzz allows you to create digital business cards and share them instantly via NFC. Link your Instagram, Snapchat, TikTok, and more.

How to Create an Instagram NFC Card

1

Open the App

Launch TapTheHuzz on your Android device.

2

Navigate to "All Cards"

Go to the cards section and tap the "+" button to create a new card.

3

Select Instagram

Choose "Instagram" from the list of supported platforms.

4

Enter Details

Input your Instagram username. The app will automatically generate the link.

5

Tap to Share

Select your new card and tap your phone against another NFC-enabled device to share your profile!
